Frequently Asked Questions

What’s unique about Life Partner?

Life Partner is built for serious relationships. We focus on verified profiles and meaningful connections — not casual chats.

How do I verify my profile?

Upload a photo with the required gesture. Our team reviews each request to ensure authenticity. Once approved, you’ll get your blue badge.

Is Life Partner free to use?

Yes! All current features are free. Premium options are coming soon to enhance your experience even more.

Can I use Life Partner outside of Myanmar?

Absolutely. Our platform connects users globally — from Myanmar to Thailand, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, and beyond.

How do I report inappropriate behavior or fake accounts?

Just tap “Report” on any profile. Our team reviews reports quickly to keep Life Partner safe and respectful.

What devices support the app?

Life Partner is available on both iOS and Android. Download it today on the App Store or Google Play.
